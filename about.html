About Me

Hello! I’m Anditi Grace Eva, a Telecommunications and Information Engineering student at JKUAT with a strong passion for Industry Innovation and Infrastructure. I am also actively pursuing Software Engineering to broaden my skill set and stay at the forefront of technological advancements.

With a keen interest in Broadcast Engineering and a commitment to empowering women in tech, I currently serve as the Vice Chair of Women in Tech at JKUAT. My journey involves practical experience in broadcasting, satellite communication, and a growing expertise in web development, coding, and system optimization.

Beyond technical skills, I value soft skills such as leadership, collaboration, effective communication, and problem-solving which are essential for driving impactful projects and working in diverse teams.

I am driven by a mission to contribute to Kenya’s technology infrastructure through innovation, while inspiring fellow women to take up space in the tech industry.
